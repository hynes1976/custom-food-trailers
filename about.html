Our Story

Bespoke food trailers that pair nostalgic design with modern engineering, tailored to each client's requirements.

How it began

From a coffee roastery to bespoke food trailers

It all started in the heart of the Lake District, when Rinaldo's Coffee Roastery rolled out its first Airstream style coffee trailer. What began as a passion for great coffee and beautiful design soon turned into a new challenge, finding food trailers that were reliable, well built, and full of character.

After struggling to source quality units that met both UK standards and our own high expectations, we set out to change that. That's how Custom Food Trailers was born, out of a desire to bring craftsmanship, style, and practicality together in one polished package, and to make sourcing the right trailer simple for others.

Where we are today

Trailers that turn heads and tell stories

Today, we supply trailers that don't just serve food, they turn heads and tell stories. Each one is built using ISO 9001 certified materials and components, meeting UK and EU Whole Vehicle Type Approval for total peace of mind.

From 32 to 64 amp electrical systems and AL-KO frames and brakes, to fully equipped sinks, extractors, and refrigeration, every detail is chosen with reliability and performance in mind. Whether it's a retro Airstream coffee bar, a sleek mobile kitchen, or a one of a kind build, our goal is simple: to help you stand out, serve proudly, and hit the road with confidence.
