Common questions

Food Trailer FAQs

How much does a custom food trailer cost?

Our custom food trailers start from £14,950 for a compact 3m build and rise to around £21,950 for a 7m trailer, before options such as premium finishes, cooking equipment and power upgrades. You can see live pricing on our online builder.

How long does it take to get a food trailer?

Lead time depends on your chosen size, specification and current availability. Once you send an enquiry we confirm a timescale for your trailer, so please get in touch for the current schedule.

Is finance available for a food trailer?

Yes. Finance is available subject to status and affordability, with representative rates from 7.9% APR. You can start a finance application on our Build Your Own page.

Are your food trailers road legal and compliant?

Every trailer is built to UK and EU standards, including Whole Vehicle Type Approval, and made with ISO 9001 certified materials and components. Trailers include AL-KO chassis, braking and traction frames for safe, legal towing.

Do you deliver food trailers across the UK?

Yes. We supply bespoke trailers tailored to your requirements and deliver to customers across the whole of the UK.

What is included as standard?

Every trailer includes internal and external LED lighting, stainless steel counters, an under counter fridge and freezer, twin sinks with hot and cold water, water tanks, a gas storage box, double skin insulation and a full AL-KO chassis, among other standard features.
